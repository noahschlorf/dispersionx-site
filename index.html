Quick Answers

  • Logging shots: Tap the club pills across the top, enter intended distance, carry, and lateral miss. Use the contact picker to note thin/fat/toe hits.
  • Filters: The calendar chip lets you pick presets (Last 30 / 90 days) or define a custom range.
  • Aim suggestions: Available once you have 30 shots per club. They update automatically after each new entry.
  • Data privacy: Everything stays on-device by default. Turn on iCloud backup in Settings if you want synced data.
