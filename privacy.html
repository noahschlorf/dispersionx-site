Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 26, 2025

DispersionX is built to be a no-noise, data-driven golf companion. We respect your privacy and collect the minimum necessary information to keep the app working. This policy explains what we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Store

  • Shot data: clubs, distances, lateral dispersion, notes, and timestamps you log in the app. Stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences: filters, units, default clubs, and visualization toggles so DispersionX stays configured the way you like.

Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud backup in Settings, your data will be stored securely in your personal iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit. DispersionX does not see, collect, or transmit that data to our servers.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account sign-up or login.
  • No location, contacts, or calendar access.
  • No analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple frameworks (SwiftUI, Core Data, CloudKit) which comply with Apple’s platform security and privacy standards. We do not integrate external analytics or advertising SDKs.

Your Choices

  • Export / Delete: Contact privacy@dispersionx.app if you need help exporting or removing data.
  • Disable iCloud: Turn off the iCloud toggle in-app at any time to keep data strictly on-device.

Updates

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. For major updates we will also notify in-app.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or security, reach out at privacy@dispersionx.app.
